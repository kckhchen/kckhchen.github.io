My MSc Dissertation

My MSc dissertation, under the supervision of Dr. Ritabrata Dutta, is titled "Probabilistic Weather Forecasting with Recursive Algorithms and Vine Copulas". It explores the possibility of applying an efficient nonparametric recursive marginal density estimation method to time-series data and utilising vine copulas, a statistical tool to model multi-dimensional copulas and evaluate their densities, to estimate complex nonlinear temporal dependence structures in time series. In this dissertation, the algorithm has been proven to be effective in making probabilistic forecasts on geopotential weather data.
